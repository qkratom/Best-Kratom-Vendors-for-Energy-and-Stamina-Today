## How to Dose Kratom for Maximum Energy

Dosing strategy matters as much as strain selection for energy effects.

**Lower doses (1-3 grams) tend to produce the most stimulating effects.** This is counterintuitive for new users who assume more is better, but kratom's dose-response relationship is well-documented in pharmacological discussions of its alkaloid activity.

**Higher doses (5+ grams) typically shift effects toward sedation regardless of strain color.** A white vein strain at a high dose can feel more relaxing than energizing, which is why starting low is especially important for energy-focused use.

**Time of day affects perceived effects.** Most users report the strongest energy response when taking white or green strains in the morning on an empty stomach, with effects lasting several hours into the workday.

**Avoid daily same-strain use.** Rotating between different white and green strains is widely recommended in kratom communities to maintain sensitivity to energizing effects over time.
